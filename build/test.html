Mac and Cheese

By Brian Koser, June 24, 2014 This is cheesy. Also mac. Yield: 2 bowls Ingredients:
  • 3 or 4 ripe bananas, smashed
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Mix in the ingredients in a bowl. Add the flour last.
  2. Pour the mixture into a loaf pan and bake for one hour.
